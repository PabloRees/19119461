\begin{abstract}
\small{
An analysis of the effects of Covid 19 follows. It was found that Africa
had lower vaccination rates and an accompanying higher death rate
relative to infection rates than the rest of the world. Further it was
found that higher rates of smoking had a high correlation
(\textasciitilde30\%) with ICU admission rates and by extension, death
rates. Finally, it was found that no single country managed to increase
the number of hospital beds during the pandemic - or rather, it was
found that the data may have some mistakes in it.
}
\end{abstract}
